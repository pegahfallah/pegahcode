# Mali: Link in Bio Platform for Creators

## Overview

Mali is a link-in-bio SaaS platform designed to help creators and businesses build personalized landing pages. The platform offers a modern solution for consolidating and showcasing digital presence through customizable, responsive pages that support international audiences. With built-in analytics and dynamic link customization, Mali helps creators maximize their online impact.

## Key Features

### Dynamic Page Builder
Intuitive drag-and-drop interface for creating personalized landing pages with custom themes and layouts.

### International Support
Built-in internationalization support enabling creators to reach global audiences in their preferred languages.

### Analytics Dashboard
Comprehensive tracking and analytics to help creators understand visitor behavior and optimize conversion rates.

### Custom Branding
Flexible customization options allowing users to maintain consistent branding across their digital presence.
